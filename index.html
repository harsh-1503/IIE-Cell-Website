WCE INNOVATION INCUBATION AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP CELL

Nurturing Innovations Creating Enterprises

The Innovation Incubation and Entrepreneurship Cell (IIEC) at Walchand College of Engineering is a hub of creativity, innovation, and entrepreneurial spirit. The IIEC was established with the objective of nurturing and supporting the next generation of entrepreneurs and innovators by providing them with the resources, guidance, and support they need to turn their ideas into successful businesses.

Enrollment Learn More

Objectives

The institute has setup the IIE Cell during the academic year 2015-16 with the following objectives:

Create the awareness among WCE faculty members and studentsfor innovation, incubation and entrepreneurship.

provide platform for promotion of ideas/projects to product development / commercialization.

provide the pre-incubation space for ideation and prototyping

Nurture the pre-incubates for startup

Assist the pre-incubates for getting funds / financial support under various Govt. schemes, from venture capitalist, industries.
